Citing OpenSTEF#

If you are using OpenSTEF in your research work, please consider citing our library as below. You can cite the project using the DOI 10.5281/zenodo.15316405 or reference the GitHub repository at OpenSTEF/openstef.

BibTeX Format#

@software{openstefopenstef,
  title = {OpenSTEF/openstef},
  author = {van den Bogaard, Jonas and van Es, Daan and Pleiter, Bart and van Doorn, Jan Maarten and Stoel, Willem Frederik and de Smet, Clara and Dmitriev, Egor and Schilders, Lars and Kreuwel, Frank and Harmsen, A. W. and Fortin, Maxime},
  doi = {10.5281/zenodo.15316405},
  url = {https://github.com/OpenSTEF/openstef},
}
